Scheuer Family

    Jacob and Sabine Boscowitz' youngest daugther Minna married Max Rosenthal. Together they had two children Hedwig (1904 - 1993) and Manfred (1907 - 1975). Hedwig married Sally Scheuer (1893 - 1992) in September of 1929. They had one son Ernest, born July 28, 1930, in Bad Neuheim. Enclosed are a diary of their immigration to the United States, and a memoir of their life in America.
