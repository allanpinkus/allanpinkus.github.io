Sabine Boscowitz Pinkus

Jacob and Sabine Boscowitz' son Albert (1878 - 1938) was a partner with his father and brother-in-law Gustav Rebitzer (1870 - 1943) (married to Albert's sister Ernestine (Tina) (1875 - 1942)) in the company "Jacob Boscowitz und Co." in Weiden, that was both a retail and wholesale shoe business. They built a new shoe factory in 1926. Here is a photo of the factory (right click on it to enlarge).



After serving in the German army in WWI, Albert married Johanna Oppenheimer (1886 - 1942) on December 1, 1919. They had one daughter Sabine (1921 - 2016) (named after her grandmother). Here are her "Memories".
