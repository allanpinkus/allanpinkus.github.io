The Spaeth Family and Ottensoos

    Sabine's paternal grandparents were Jacob Boscowitz (1844-1930) and Sabine Späth (1851-1917), after whom she was named. Sabine Späth (spelled in English Spaeth) was from Ottensoos, a small town about 10 km northeast of Nürnberg. The Rebitzer and Prager families were also from there. We are related, by marriage, to both. There was a fair degree of intermarriage. Jacob's sister Gella (1841 - 1880) married Hermann Prager (1833 - 1913), moved to Ottensoos and bore him 12 children. Jacob and Sabine's eldest daughter Ernestine (Tina) (1875 - 1942) married Gustav Rebitzer (1870 - 1943) from Ottensoos. Gustav moved to Weiden and was a partner with his father-in-law Jacob, and his brother-in-law Albert in "Jacob Boscowitz and Co."
