Further Boscowitz Stories


    Here are a few things that I have discovered regarding other branches of the Bosc(k)owitz family from Floss. These are all descendants of Aron Boskowitz (1720 - 1784) and Rachel Ploch who lived in Floss. Although it seems that Aron emigrated there from Boskovice in Moravia (now part of the Czech Republic), hence the family name.
